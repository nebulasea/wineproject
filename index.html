Educational Resources

    GuildSomm

    GuildSomm is an excellent website for both budding and advanced oenophiles. Although most of the website is membership based, there are plenty of great public-accessible videos, podcasts, and articles.



    The Wine Bible by Karen MacNeil

    Karen MacNeil's book is a must-have for any level of wine enthusiast. Informative and a joy to read, you easily jump to any section to read up varietals and countries.



    Winefolly

    A great book to have on hand, and a very educational and fun blog. Especially if you would like to know more about wine and food pairings.



    Court of Master Sommeliers America

    This link is for those of you, that want to take your education to the next level and sign up for certified sommelier exams.
